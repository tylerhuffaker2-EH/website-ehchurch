Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 14, 2025

Introduction

RootedLife ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Health Data

  • Weight and fitness data from Apple Health (with your permission)
  • Nutrition information you manually enter or scan via barcode
  • Activity and exercise data you choose to track

Personal Information

  • Account information (email, username) if you create an account
  • User preferences and app settings
  • Progress data related to your wellness goals

Technical Information

  • Device information (iOS version, device model) for compatibility
  • App usage analytics to improve functionality
  • Crash reports to fix bugs and improve stability

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide app functionality (tracking progress, displaying charts)
  • Sync data with Apple Health (only with your permission)
  • Improve app performance and fix issues
  • Provide customer support when needed

Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage on your device for core app functionality
  • Cloud storage (Firebase) for account data and sync across devices
  • Encryption of sensitive data both in transit and at rest
  • No third-party sharing of personal health information

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) for user authentication and data sync
  • FatSecret API for food nutrition database (no personal data shared)
  • Apple Health integration (data stays on your device unless you choose to sync)

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and control your health data through Apple Health
  • Delete your account and associated data at any time
  • Opt out of analytics and crash reporting
  • Control permissions for health data access

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the Effective Date.

Data Retention

  • Health data: Retained as long as you use the app or until you delete it
  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Analytics data: Anonymized and retained for app improvement purposes

International Users

If you are using our app from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.
