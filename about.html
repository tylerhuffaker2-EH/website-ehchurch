About Everlasting Hope Church

Growing daily in Jesus.

We are a Christ-centered, Bible-believing church dedicated to sharing God's truth, strengthening faith, and building a community rooted in love and hope.

Our Mission

Glorify God. Reach the lost. Equip believers.

We pursue personal transformation one life at a time. Through faithful teaching, prayer, and discipleship, we help people know Jesus and walk in His calling.

“Give me your lantern and compass, give me a map, so I can find my way to the sacred mountain, to the place of your presence...”

Psalm 43:3

What We Value

A church family rooted in Scripture and love.

Biblical Teaching

We teach God's Word faithfully and practically for everyday life.

Authentic Community

We care for one another, pray together, and walk through life together.

Local Outreach

We serve our neighbors and seek to share the hope of Christ in Knoxville.

Discipleship for All Ages

From children to adults, we invest in spiritual growth across generations.
