# COMPLIANCE

This document serves as the required markdown form for the project and is intended to record the statements mandated by the Technology Ministry compliance policy.

## Required Statements

### AI Usage
Artificial intelligence tools were utilized during portions of the website’s development process, including technical guidance, code suggestions, and documentation support. All AI‑generated content was reviewed, edited, and approved by human developers under the authority of Everlasting Hope Church. AI served only as an aid and did not replace human oversight or final decision‑making.

### Developed by Tyler Huffaker
The website was developed by Tyler Huffaker, President of Everlasting Hope Church, through the church’s Technology Ministry. All design, implementation, and content reflect the ministry’s mission and are subject to church leadership review and approval.

### Open Source
This website is released as an open‑source project to provide transparency in its development and to encourage collaboration where appropriate. Source code and design assets are available for community review, though any use of branding or ministry materials remains subject to church permission.

### Follows Compliance Rules
The project adheres to the Technology Ministry’s compliance requirements, including but not limited to:

- Respect for intellectual property and copyright.
- Protection of personal data and privacy in accordance with church policies and applicable laws.
- Child safety protocols for youth‑facing content.
- Accessibility and security best practices.
- Doctrinal consistency and adherence to church teachings.
- Disclosure of AI usage and proper developer attribution.

These standards ensure the website operates with integrity, transparency, and accountability as part of the church’s digital ministry.
