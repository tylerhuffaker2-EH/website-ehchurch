FAQs

Common RootedLife questions.

What is RootedLife?

RootedLife is a free life‑improvement app created by Everlasting Hope Church to help people grow spiritually, mentally, and physically. It combines devotionals, wellness tracking, journaling, and guided plans in one place.

Who is RootedLife for?

RootedLife is for everyone. Whether you are growing in faith, building healthier habits, or simply wanting a more intentional routine, the app is designed to meet you where you are.

Do I have to be religious to use RootedLife?

No. While the app was built on Christian values, spiritual features can be toggled on or off. You can still use all of the mental and physical growth tools independently.

Is RootedLife really free?

Yes. RootedLife will always be free with no ads, no paid upgrades, and no subscriptions. It is a ministry of Everlasting Hope Church.

What features are included?

The full release includes water and exercise tracking, stretching routines, weight and mood logs, daily affirmations, devotionals, Scripture reflections, health assessments, and customizable routines.

How do I get the app?

RootedLife is available now on the Apple App Store. Search for “RootedLife” or tap the link below.

Download on the App Store →
Is there an Android version?

An Android release is in development and is expected in the coming months. Sign up for updates through the app or contact us if you'd like to be notified.

Who developed RootedLife?

RootedLife was developed by Tyler Huffaker under the auspices of Everlasting Hope Church. It’s built in Flutter and fully owned and maintained by the church.

How can I suggest features or report a problem?

Use the feedback form inside the app or email rootedlife@everlastinghopechurch.org with your ideas and bug reports.

Why is it called RootedLife?

The name comes from Colossians 2:7, encouraging deep and steady growth in faith, mind, and body.
