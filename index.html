Who We Are

A Church Built on Faith, Hope & Love

Everlasting Hope Church began with a simple conviction: that every person deserves a community where they are known, loved, and equipped to live out their God-given purpose. We are a family of believers rooted in Scripture and passionate about our city.

  • Scripture-centered teaching every week
  • Authentic, multigenerational community
  • Hands-on local outreach and benevolence
  • Technology & resources for ministry nonprofits
