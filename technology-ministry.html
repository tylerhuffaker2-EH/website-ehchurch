Technology Ministry

Equipping you for a digital world.

Our Technology Ministry exists to educate and empower people in coding, artificial intelligence, and other emerging technologies. We offer resources, tutorials, and community conversations that help believers steward modern tools wisely.

What We Do

Learning, building, and understanding together.

From beginner-friendly coding workshops to discussions on ethical AI usage, our team creates accessible content for all skill levels. Whether you're curious about web development, interested in machine learning, or simply want a safer online experience, we have something for you.

We also maintain RootedLife, a digital discipleship tool designed to foster daily growth -- just one of several projects that fall under our technological outreach.
